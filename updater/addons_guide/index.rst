##########################
Addons Guidelines
##########################

Making a addon Updater compatible is pretty easy. You just need to use a compatible directory structure and most of the time it will just work.


Addon Detection Methods
=========================
When an Addon is uploaded Updater goes ahead and unzips the package. Then we try to detect the addon name, version number and type.
